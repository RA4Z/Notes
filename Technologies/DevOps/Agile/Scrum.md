Scrum methodology is a project management framework that combines the agile philosophy with the Scrum framework to deliver products incrementally

It uses [[Sprints]] to develop and deliver a working product

### Roles:
- **Product Owner:** Maximizes the value of the product and is responsible for the Backlog
- **Scrum Master:** Ensure Scrum is understood and coach the team removing impediments
- **Developers:** The team that build the product
