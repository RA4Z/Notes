A Process of verifying and validating whether the Software, Product or Application is working as expected or not.

### Aspects of Software Testing
- **Verification and Validation:** Testing involves verification, ensuring that the software meets the requirements, and validation, ensuring that the software meets the user's needs and expectations
- **Defect Detection:** Discovering bugs, errors and issues within the software before it is released
- **Quality Assurance:** Analyzing the overall quality, improving reliability, performance, security and usability.
